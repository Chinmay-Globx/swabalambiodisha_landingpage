About Us

Swabalambi Odisha is a mission-driven initiative dedicated to nurturing and empowering the entrepreneurial spirit of Odisha. We support entrepreneurs, MSMEs, and startups by creating an enabling ecosystem that fosters innovation, growth, and sustainability. At the heart of our work lies a commitment to local business empowerment. Through regular workshops, training programs, networking events, and monthly connects, we bring together a vibrant community of changemakers, mentors, investors, and industry experts. By bridging critical gaps in resources, networks, and opportunities, Swabalambi Odisha strives to make entrepreneurship a viable and rewarding path for all. Together, we are building a self-reliant, vibrant, and inclusive entrepreneurial ecosystem for the state. Be part of the movement to transform Odisha into a thriving centre of innovation and entrepreneurship.
